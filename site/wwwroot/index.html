Black Ops Gunfight

Call of Duty: Black Ops 1 · PC

Get Started Discord GitHub

01 //About

Two teams. One shared loadout that rotates every other round — no health regen, no custom classes, no killstreaks, just raw gunplay on an even field. When the clock runs out, capture the overtime flag to steal the round; if nobody caps, the team with the most remaining health takes it. First to 6 round wins takes the match.

02 //In-game

Everyone runs the same kit, and if the timer runs dry with both teams alive, health decides it.

03 //How to play

Plutonium auto-downloads the mod when you connect — no manual install. Just launch Black Ops through the Plutonium client, open the Server Browser, and join Gunfight (gunfight.us).
