Call of Duty: Black Ops 1 · PC

01 //About

Black Ops Gunfight brings the authentic Gunfight game mode to the original Call of Duty: Black Ops.

Two teams face off using a shared loadout that rotates every other round. No health regeneration, no custom loadouts, no killstreaks.

If time expires, capture the overtime flag to secure the round. Otherwise, the team with the most remaining health wins the round. The first team to win 6 rounds wins the match.

02 //How to play

Plutonium auto-downloads the mod when you connect — no manual install. Just launch Black Ops through the Plutonium client, open the Server Browser, and join Gunfight (gunfight.us).

Requirements

  1. 1

    Download Plutonium.

  2. 2

    A copy of Black Ops from Steam or from our Discord.
